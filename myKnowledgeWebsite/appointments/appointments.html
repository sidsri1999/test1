UIDAI's CEO: Saurabh Garg
Senior Bureaucrat Saurabh Garg has been appointed as the Chief Executive Officer(CEO) of Unique Identification Authority of India(UIDAI) as part of a bureaucratic reshuffle effected by the center in March 2021. Garg is a 1991 batch IAS officer, the appointment committee of cabinet(ACC) has approved the appointment of Garg as the CEO, UIDAI in the rank and pay scale of additional secretory.
Reference: Reference: https://thewire.in/government/saurabh-garg-appointed-uidai-ceo-sanjeev-kumar-to-head-aai-in-bureaucratic-reshuffle

AAI's Chairman: Sanjeev Kumar
Sanjeev Kumar, a 1991 batch IAS officer of Maharashtra cadre, has been appointed as the chairman of, Airports Authority of India (AAI).
Reference: https://thewire.in/government/saurabh-garg-appointed-uidai-ceo-sanjeev-kumar-to-head-aai-in-bureaucratic-reshuffle

FCI's Chairman and MD: Atish Chandra
Atish Chandra will be chairman and managing director, Food Corporation of India in the rank and pay of additional secretary, the order said. Chandra, a 1994 batch IAS officer of Bihar cadre, is currently joint secretary, Department of Agriculture, Cooperation and Farmers Welfare.

CSI: GP Samanta
In March 2021, Government appoints Dr. GP Samanta as the Chief Statistician of India, for a period of 2 years.
Samanta till then was an advisor in the RBI's Department of Statistics and Information Management.
CSI also holds the charge of the Secretary of MOSPI(Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation).
Samanta is India's fourth CSI.
